Welcome to UA's NSF-supported Research Training Group (RTG) in the Data Driven Discovery! Hosted by the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Arizona, we are a group of faculty, postdocs, and students (graduate and undergraduate) interested in modern computational methods for data driven modeling and their application to physical, biological, and engineered systems.

The RTG Seminar covers a range of topics, including expository talks, discussions of interesting papers, and the occasional formal seminar talk. The seminar welcomes participation from all members of the mathematical sciences community at the University of Arizona.
